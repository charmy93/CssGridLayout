Compensation

Bison Transport rewards its people well. We offer competitive pay and a comprehensive flexible benefits package that is regularly reviewed to ensure we are able to attract and retain high quality employees in a rapidly changing marketplace. Bison's flexible benefits plan allows you to select Health and Dental coverage that best meets your individual and family needs. We support a learning environment and are committed to developing our people and equipping them with skills that have immediate, practical application to our work environment.

Bison Transport offers a yearly profit sharing plan where employees receive a percentage of Bison's net profits, in addition to their wages.
